Biography

I am a software developer focusing on web development. Before transitioning to tech, I studied and worked in the healthcare industry as an aspiring physical therapist, but my schooling suddenly came to a halt. Fortunately, this gave me permission to reflect on my experiences, reassess my goals and aspirations, and pursue programming with a purpose. My hope is that I can leverage the skills and education I gained in the healthcare industry to benefit my community on a larger scale using software.

My previous work experiences a student physical therapist and coach have set a strong foundation for my development as a developer. In these roles, I was regularly tasked with solving problems that required critical thinking, collaboration, resourcefulness, and patience. Moreover, I thoroughly enjoyed extending my knowledge to others as a skilled professional. With that said, I intend to pursue my passion for service and learning, through teaching and mentoring opportunities to help aspiring developers in the future.
